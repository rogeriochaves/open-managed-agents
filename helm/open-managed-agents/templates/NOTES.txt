NOTE: No VAULT_ENCRYPTION_KEY was provided. The server will auto-generate one
on first boot. To persist encrypted data across restarts, extract the key
and pin it:

  kubectl exec deploy/{{ include "oma.fullname" . }}-server -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} \
    -- cat /data/.env 2>/dev/null | grep VAULT_ENCRYPTION_KEY

Then set it in your values:
